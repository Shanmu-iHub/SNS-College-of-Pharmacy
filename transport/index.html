Transport Guidelines

Important information for parents and students

For Parents

  • Punctuality: Students must report 5 minutes before bus arrival
  • Pick-up: Parents must receive the child at the bus stop
  • Changes: Route/stop changes require 1 month advance notice
  • Communication: All requests via official email only

For Students

  • Uniform: Proper college uniform mandatory while travelling
  • ID Card: College ID with bus number required at all times
  • Prohibited: Eating, drinking, or games in bus are not allowed
  • Behavior: Maintain discipline; misbehavior may cancel bus service

College Timings

Buses are scheduled to ensure students arrive by 8:45 AM daily.

Routes are designed to minimise transit time and provide comfortable journeys.
